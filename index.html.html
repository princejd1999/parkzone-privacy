Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ParkZone ("we", "our", or "us"). ParkZone is a smart parking booking application that connects parking space owners with drivers in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information you provide:

  • Full name and email address (registration)
  • Phone number
  • Vehicle number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Aadhaar card image (for owner KYC verification only)
  • Parking space photos and address (owners only)
  • UPI ID and payment QR code (owners only)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device location (to show nearby parking spaces)
  • Booking history and parking activity
  • App usage data and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process parking bookings and payments
  • To show nearby available parking spaces on the map
  • To send booking confirmations and OTP notifications
  • To verify parking space owner identity (KYC)
  • To display your profile photo to other users during a booking
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To respond to your support requests and complaints

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your information may be shared in these limited cases:

  • Between users: Drivers can see the parking owner's name and photo during a booking. Owners can see the driver's name, phone number, and photo for an active booking.
  • Firebase / Google: We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and storage. Firebase's privacy policy applies.
  • Legal requirement: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase Cloud Firestore servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Profile photos, parking photos, and KYC documents are stored as encrypted data. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data including parking spaces, bookings, and photos are permanently deleted.

6. Location Data

ParkZone requests access to your device's location to:

  • Show parking spaces near you on the map
  • Help you navigate to your booked parking space
  • Allow parking owners to set their space location accurately

Location is only accessed while the app is in use. We do not track your location in the background. You can disable location access in your device settings, but some app features may not work correctly.

7. Children's Privacy

ParkZone is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct your personal information (via Profile → Edit Profile)
  • Delete your account and all associated data (via Profile → Delete Account)
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
